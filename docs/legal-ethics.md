# Legal and Ethical Use

This code is provided for educational and research purposes. You must ensure any use complies with applicable law, platform terms, and ethical standards.

## Minimum requirements for any use
- **Informed consent:** Every account/device you monitor must belong to you or to someone who has explicitly authorized you in writing.
- **Lawful basis:** Verify that monitoring is permitted under your jurisdiction (privacy, wiretap, computer misuse, data protection laws).
- **Data minimization:** Collect only what you need; avoid retaining identifiers longer than necessary.
- **Transparency:** Be clear with participants about what is collected, how long it is kept, and who can access it.
- **Security controls:** Protect stored SQLite data and auth material; restrict access; rotate credentials.

## Prohibited uses
- Surveillance of individuals without consent.
- Circumventing platform policies or protections.
- Combining this data with other datasets to profile or track people without their knowledge.

If you have any doubt about legality or ethics, **do not deploy this project.**
